Silent – Secure Messaging App

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Silent (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Silent mobile application (“App”) handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Silent does not collect:

  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • User names
  • Contacts
  • Device identifiers for tracking

1.2 Messages and Communication

  • All messages are protected using end-to-end encryption.
  • Messages are encrypted on the sender’s device and decrypted only on the recipient’s device.
  • We cannot read, access, store, or monitor message content.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

Silent may collect limited non-personal technical data such as app version and anonymous crash logs. This data cannot be used to identify users.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide secure messaging functionality
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Fix bugs and crashes

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Industry-standard encryption protects all communications
  • Message content is not stored in readable form on servers
  • Reasonable safeguards prevent unauthorized access

4. Third-Party Services

Silent uses the following service:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – for infrastructure, real-time messaging, and stability monitoring

5. Children’s Privacy

Silent is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

This Privacy Policy may be updated when required. Updates will be reflected on this page.
